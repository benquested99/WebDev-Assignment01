Amimations

  • Fade In
    • The 'Fade In' animation is used on the content of every page, excluding the navigation bar. It increases the opacity from 0% to 100% over the time span of 1.5 seconds.

  • Slide in from Bottom
    • The 'Slide in from Bottom' animation slides the CSS element onto the page from the bottom, over a duration of 1.5 seconds. This is done by altering the top margin from 20%, down to 1%. In this case, the animation is applied to the image on the Home landing page.
